Recruiting Mission

November 2-4, 2020

VanHack Recruiting Missions is back for its first stop in Africa!


Following the surge in tech talenst in Africa, Vanhack is going all the way to Accra, Ghana for this year's Vanhack Recruitment Mission on 2nd to 4th November 2020.


This is an amazing chance to do face-to-face interviews with international companies and land a job abroad. Don’t miss it!


Who Can Apply


If you have three or more of professional experience, advanced or fluent English, Availability to attend the Recruiting Mission Event on 2nd to 4th November in person and the desire to get a job in Canada or Europe and to relocate in the first semester of 2021

How to apply?


  1. Apply to the event here

  2. Update your Profile

  3. Complete the English Verification

  4. Complete the code Verification
